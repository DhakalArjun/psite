Metrology Inspector (Metrologist)
Nepal Bureau of Standards & Metrology(NBSM),
Under Ministry of Industries, Government of Nepal,
Kathmandu, Nepal
Main Responsibility:
  • To perform calibration of different measuring instruments used for mass, force, pressure, volume, dimension, temperature, and humidity measurements.
  • Perform all activities to ensure compliance with ISO17025 requirements.
  • Develop application or calculation programs for measurement and estimation of measurement uncertainties as per international standards for different measurement units.
  • For continual improvement, prepare or revise technical procedures, calculation applications or programs, and quality documentation.
  • Provide input to top management regarding the expansion of the scope of laboratories, purchase of new equipment, and participate in different committees formed by the bureau.
As a Metrology Inspector, participate in market surveillance or other inspection activities to regulate The Standard Weight & Measure Act and Rules of Nepal.
